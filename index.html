What is logout about

LOGOUT is the largest conference of Information technology, computer science technology, gaming etc. within Lithuania. It’s a 2 day conference which involves newest trends in development and technology. Last year it involved 2,000 professionals, enthusiasts and stakeholders engaging in workshops, discussing relevant issues, networking, giving out awards and providing Vilnius with a solid party. We are proud to be able to welcome you all again this year as previous success and unexpected involvement of people last year encouraged us to try even harder to showcase newest trends and involve as many new enthusiasts as possible....
